A short story on how did I start gaming and drawing

My love for gaming started when I was just 4 years old as I saw my father playing in the living room.

I would sit next to him and he would then pass me the controller even though I didn't know what I was doing.

Later on as I grew up, my dad and I would compete against each other and we would end up fighting because one of us always cheated.

As time passed he grew busy and we didn't play as much, but I continued on my own.

Another way my dad and I spent time together was drawing.

He never took classes, it was like a gift and apparently I also had this gift.

Just the same as gaming we would fight on who drew better, we choosed categories to draw and my mom would be the judge.

Drawing and Gaming are the best way I pass the time, not only do I play with my dad but I also compete with my friends.
